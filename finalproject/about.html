Who are we?

Bountiful foods is a California based drink company that uses local fresh produce to create delicious smoothies. There are plenty of fruits to choose from allowing near infinite amount of fruit smoothie combinations.

Bountiful foods also delivers their fresh local farm produce to most coastal cities in California. Our goal is to make it simple to get fresh local produce delivered to your home so you can live a healthier lifestyle!

Our first location is in the beautiful Santa Barbara, California. Design your first drink and drop by to get a 30% off coupon!
