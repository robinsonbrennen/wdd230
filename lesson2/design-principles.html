White Space and Clean Design

Microsoft

microsoft.com

The whitespace use and clean design of the Microsoft homepage gives a sense of cleanliness and order. The space they do use has very clear actions and contrasting colors that help with the overall aesthetic.

PARC: Alignment

United States of America Government

usa.gov

Upon opening the usa.gov webpage there is a direct line progression down the middle of the page. There is even an arrow pointing towards where the line is headed while being right along that same line.

PARC: Repetition

Fabletics

fabletics.com

Fabletics does a great job in keeping to 2 styles and less. While they keep to two styles they use many different ways to add variety to those styles like bolding, changing the color, and changing the size.
